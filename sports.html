1. The term ‘ Putt’ is used in the sport ______ ?

Cricket

Table Tennis

Golf

Football

Answer: Golf

Explanation:

The term ‘Putt’ is used in the sport Golf. Miniature golf, also known as minigolf, mini-putt, crazy golf, or putt-putt, is an offshoot of the sport of golf focusing solely on the putting aspect of its parent game.
